Our story

Built by hand.
Rooted in the beach.

Moks Café wasn't born from a business plan. It was built — literally — board by board, post by post, right here on the shores of Cadiz City. The crew raised this house on the beach themselves, and it's been feeding locals, welcoming strangers, and vibing ever since.

Since 2019, this spot has been the real deal: where the community gathers, where the rice is cooked right, where you can pitch a tent and fall asleep to the sound of waves. No fancy resort nonsense. Just good food, cold drinks, and genuine Filipino warmth.

And if you want to see more of the Philippines? The owners have you covered — they run private tours of the region, the kind that only locals can give you.
